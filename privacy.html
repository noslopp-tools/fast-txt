This privacy policy applies to the Fast_TXT Android application.

1. Introduction

Fast_TXT is a text expansion utility that helps users type faster by automatically expanding short trigger phrases into longer text snippets. This document explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: Email address and Google account identifier used for authentication and recovery.

User content: Your snippets, shared group data, and group share codes.

Usage data: Snippet expansion counts, timestamps, character savings, and app-level usage statistics.

Device identifier: A randomly generated identifier used to enforce subscription device limits.

3. Accessibility Service Disclosure

On Android, Fast_TXT uses an Accessibility Service to detect trigger text in editable fields so it can expand saved snippets.

  • The service monitors editable text fields to detect your configured triggers.
  • The service is not intended to store or transmit everything you type.
  • You can disable the Accessibility Service at any time in Android Settings.

4. Clipboard Access

The {clipboard} placeholder reads clipboard content only when explicitly used by a snippet expansion. Clipboard data is not stored for unrelated use.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account recovery
  • Syncing snippets across devices
  • Group sharing features
  • Usage statistics and product diagnostics
  • Device limit enforcement

6. Data Storage and Security

Fast_TXT uses Google Firebase services for authentication, data storage, and backend operations. Data is transmitted over TLS. We do not sell or rent your personal data.

7. Data Sharing

Your data is shared only when necessary to provide the service, such as storing data on Firebase or showing group content to other members of a group you joined.

8. Your Rights

  • Access and correction of your data
  • Account deletion from within the app
  • Withdrawal of Accessibility Service consent by disabling it in Android Settings

9. Children’s Privacy

Fast_TXT is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after material changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact support@fasttxt.app.
